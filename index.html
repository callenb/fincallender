Fin Callender is a 21-year-old Electronic Dance Music Producer from Wokingham, Berkshire, and is currently based in Hull.

Debuting with his Future Rave track ‘Make Me Feel’, Fin Callender is eager to grow his sound and production to greater levels going forward whilst also expanding it to further push his skills into other electronic dance music genres and beyond.
